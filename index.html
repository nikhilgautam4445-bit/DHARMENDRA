1. Force Gurkha

The Force Gurkha is an off-road vehicle made and built with a military-inspired appearance.
Packed with a factory-fitted snorkel and mechanically locking differentials, the Gurkha is an off-roader for extreme usage.



specifications:
  • ENGINE:2.6L Diesel
  • POWER:91 hp @ 3200 rpm
  • TORQUE: 250 Nm @ 1400-24000 rpm

2. Mahindra Thar

The new generation Mahindra Thar is the most well-known lifestyle off-roader in India.
It is rugged, with a built-for-purpose chassis, 4×4 drivetrain, and off-roading capabilities with modern features.


specifications:
  • ENGINE:2.0L perol/ 2.2L Diesel
  • POWER: 150 hp(petrol),130 hp(diesel)
  • TORQUE:300-320 Nm
